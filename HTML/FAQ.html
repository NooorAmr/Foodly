Frequently Asked Questions

How do I place an order?

You can browse the menu, choose your meals, and add them to the cart. Then proceed to checkout.

Do you offer home delivery?

Yes, we deliver to all nearby areas within a 10 km range.

What payment methods are accepted?

We accept cash on delivery and online card payments.

Can I cancel my order?

Yes, you can cancel your order within 10 minutes after placing it.

How long does the delivery take after placing an order?

Delivery usually takes between 25 to 45 minutes depending on your location and restaurant preparation time.

What should I do if the delivery person was rude?

If the delivery person was rude or behaved in an unprofessional way, please contact our support team immediately. We will take action and ensure this does not happen again.

What should I do if the food arrived damaged or not fresh?

You can report the issue through our support section or contact us directly. We will replace the order or issue a refund based on the situation.
